The Venue
Tuesday, December 27th, 2016

Try searching the internet for wedding venues in and around Red Deer… there aren’t too many options. We decided on an outdoor space, so that meant hotels were out. We discovered a beautiful location called “The Barn” just outside of Olds. Rental of the barn alone was $7,000!!! That one was definitely out. Then we zeroed in on a couple of locations. One at the Canyon Ski Hill in Red Deer and a little known location called Billyco Junction just between Blackfalds and Lacombe.
