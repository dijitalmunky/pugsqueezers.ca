More Hidden Gems

Sunday, February 19th, 2017

by Chris

In a previous post, we shared engagement photos we shot on the exterior of one of Red Deer’s hidden gems: Queen’s Diner.

Well, the outside was just the beginning. To get the full vintage feel, we shot inside the diner right at closing time as well. It was so much fun. Our photographer, Jennifer, also had them make us up an old fashioned milkshake for some extra fun shots.

Hope you enjoy!
